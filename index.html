About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Arlington, focusing on machine learning, natural language processing, and animal computational linguistics. My supervisor is Dr. Kenny Zhu, who leads our research lab, the Arlington Computational Linguistics Lab (ACL^2). Previously, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Data Science from Knox College in Galesburg, Illinois.
